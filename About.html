The CREWMATES


Loco 2-23 is a subscription-based company. Our subscription comes with three mystery packages shipped randomly per month. Our goal is to create a fun brand that pushes the boundaries of professionalism. We hope our brand and monthly packages remind people to be outlandish and have FUN. Loco 2-23 is inspired by Mischief. Mischief is a brand of rebellion and mystery, but most importantly it's fun and creative. Similar to what my crew and I created.
